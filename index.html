Our Mission

To help companies create their own VoiceBrain, a company data repository of voice information, that produces insights to enable business efficiency and cost savings.

Why isn't there a system of records for voice and how VoiceBrain’s AI powered platform will serve up valuable information to drive efficiency.

There has never been a system of record for voice. Most companies have a system of record for their financials, manufacturing, product sales, customers, human resources, project management and more, but why not voice? The technology did not exist to provide the analysis until today. VoiceBrain’s AI powered platform will drive more informed data driven decisions.
